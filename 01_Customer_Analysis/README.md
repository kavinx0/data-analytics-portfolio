# Customer Purchase & Retention Analysis

## Overview
This project analyses transactional retail data to understand revenue trends, customer purchasing behaviour, and revenue concentration across customers and products.

The analysis focuses on identifying key drivers of revenue and assessing the importance of customer retention in overall business performance.

---

## Objectives
- Analyse revenue trends over time
- Distinguish between repeat and one-time customers
- Evaluate revenue contribution by customer type
- Identify top-performing products and customers
- Generate actionable business insights

---

## Project Flow
- Load and inspect raw transactional data
- Clean and validate data by removing invalid transactions
- Perform time-based revenue analysis
- Analyse repeat vs one-time customer behaviour
- Identify top customers and products by revenue
- Summarise insights and business implications

---

## Dataset
- Online Retail transactional dataset (UK-based)
- Contains order-level purchase data including customer identifiers, product details, quantities, prices, and transaction dates

---

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Jupyter Notebook
- Matplotlib

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue shows noticeable variation over time, suggesting seasonal or demand-driven patterns.
- Repeat customers contribute a disproportionately higher share of total revenue compared to one-time customers.
- Revenue is concentrated among a small subset of customers and products.
- Improving customer retention could significantly enhance overall revenue stability and growth.
